# Section Content Patterns Reference

Agent-readable reference with content patterns for all 7 landing page sections, organized by vertical (Lead Gen, SaaS, Ecommerce).

---

## Section 1: Hero Patterns

### Required Elements

| Element | Purpose | Constraint |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Primary headline | Core value proposition | Under 10 words, outcome-focused |
| Sub-headline | Expand with specifics | Under 2 sentences, don't repeat headline |
| Visual element | Reinforce the message | Product shot, demo video, or transformation image |
| Primary CTA | Tell visitor what to do | Benefit-driven text, contrasting color |

### Hero by Vertical

| Vertical | Visual Approach | Headline Focus | Sub-headline Focus |
|----------|----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Lead Gen** | Client results dashboard, team photo, process diagram | Outcome the service delivers | Mechanism or social proof |
| **SaaS** | Product screenshot, demo GIF, interface preview | Time/cost saved or capability unlocked | Free trial offer or social proof |
| **Ecommerce** | Hero product shot (lifestyle context), video | Product benefit or transformation | Risk removal (returns, shipping, warranty) |

### Hero Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| "Welcome to Our Platform" | Lead with the outcome or transformation |
| Generic stock photo | Use real product shots, team photos, or results |
| No CTA above the fold | Primary CTA must be visible without scrolling |
| Feature-focused headline | Translate every feature into a benefit |
| No message match with ad | Mirror ad headline language in LP headline |

## Section 2: Benefits Patterns

### Three Benefit Angles

| Angle | Focus | Lead Gen | SaaS | Ecommerce |
|-------|-------|----------|------|-----------|
| **Functional** | Time, cost, ease, results | "Save 5 hours per week on reporting" | "Deploy in 3 clicks, not 3 sprints" | "Charges in 30 minutes, lasts all day" |
| **Business** | Efficiency, resources, compliance | "Reduce acquisition costs by 30%" | "One tool replaces your 5-tool stack" | "Free shipping on all orders, every time" |
| **Personal** | Stress, confidence, recognition | "Stop worrying about pipeline at 2am" | "Look like a data genius in your next meeting" | "Compliments guaranteed or your money back" |

### Benefits Display Patterns

| Pattern | When to Use | Structure |
|---------|------------|-----------|
| **Icon + headline + description** | 3-6 benefits, visual scanning | Grid of benefit cards |
| **Before/after comparison** | Transformation-focused offers | Two-column table or visual |
| **Numbered list** | Process or methodology benefits | Sequential numbered items |
| **Feature-benefit pairs** | Technical products that need translation | Two-column: feature on left, benefit on right |

### FAB (Feature → Advantage → Benefit) Examples

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit (use on page) |
|---------|-----------|----------------------|
| 24/7 support chat | Get help anytime | "Never be stuck with an issue, even at midnight" |
| AI-powered analytics | Automatic insights | "Know exactly where to optimize without manual analysis" |
| 100-night trial | Try before you commit | "Sleep on it for 100 nights. If you don't love it, we take it back" |

## Section 3: Trust/Authority Patterns

### Trust Elements by Type

| Type | Examples | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Institutional** | ISO certifications, SOC 2, GDPR badges, SSL, industry associations | Finance, healthcare, legal, B2B enterprise |
| **Expertise** | Awards, media mentions ("As seen in"), credentials, years in business, results | Services, consulting, marketing, tech |
| **Scale** | Customer count, revenue processed, data points analyzed | SaaS, enterprise, high-volume ecommerce |

### Trust by Vertical

| Vertical | Primary Trust Approach | Examples |
|----------|----------------------|---------|
| **Lead Gen** | Expertise + results | "43% average ROI increase in 90 days," "Google Premier Partner," "15 years in PPC" |
| **SaaS** | Scale + security | "Trusted by 10,000+ teams," "SOC 2 Type 2 certified," "99.9% uptime SLA" |
| **Ecommerce** | Quality + safety | "Dermatologist tested," "Organic certified," "Made in the EU," "100,000+ orders shipped" |

### Trust Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| "We can be trusted" (vague) | "SOC 2 Type 2 certified, audited annually" (specific) |
| Fake or obscure awards | Use real, recognizable credentials only |
| Unverifiable claims | Link to supporting data or third-party source |
| Too many logos without context | Select 3-5 most recognizable, add "trusted by X companies" |

## Section 4: Social Proof Patterns

### Five Social Proof Types

| Type | Pattern | When to Use |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Testimonials** | Quote + name + photo + company | Always: place near relevant objections |
| **Case studies** | Before/after + specific metrics | Complex solutions, high-ticket, B2B |
| **Statistical proof** | "Used by 50,000 businesses" / "99.9% uptime" | SaaS, services, products with volume |
| **Expert endorsements** | Authority quote + credentials | When recognizable experts exist |
| **Third-party validation** | G2, Capterra, Trustpilot ratings + badge | SaaS, services, products with external reviews |

### Testimonial Formula

```
[Specific result] + [before state] + [after state] + [recommendation]
```

| Vertical | Weak | Strong |
|----------|------|--------|
| **Lead Gen** | "Great agency, highly recommend!" | "Pipeline went from $1.2M to $3.8M in 6 months. Before working with them, we couldn't scale past $50K/month in spend." — Sarah K., VP Marketing, TechCorp |
| **SaaS** | "Love this product!" | "Cut our reporting time from 15 hours to 45 minutes per week. Our team actually looks forward to Monday standup now." — Mike D., Head of Growth, ScaleUp Inc |
| **Ecommerce** | "Nice mattress" | "After 3 months, my back pain is gone. I've tried 4 other mattresses. This is the first one I didn't return." — James L., verified buyer |

### "Ugly Proof" Patterns (Scroll-Stoppers)

| Pattern | Example | Why It Works |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Screenshot of real chat | Slack message: "These numbers are insane" | Feels genuine, not manufactured |
| Raw dashboard screenshot | Google Ads screenshot showing 5x ROAS | Data is hard to fake, instantly credible |
| Video testimonial thumbnail | Unscripted customer on camera | Video is harder to fake than text |
| Social media post embed | Twitter/LinkedIn post praising the product | Public post is verifiable |

## Section 5: Objection Handling Patterns

### Techniques

| Technique | Pattern | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Guarantee** | "[Duration] guarantee + [simple process]" | "60-day money-back guarantee. Email us, get a refund within 48 hours. No questions, no hassle." |
| **FAQ section** | "Q: [Common concern] A: [Direct answer]" | Address top 5-7 concerns in expandable Q&A format |
| **Value framing** | Compare cost to alternatives or inaction | "At $299/month, that's less than $10/day — cheaper than the intern you'd need to do this manually" |
| **Process clarification** | Step-by-step with time estimates | "Step 1: Upload photos (5 min). Step 2: AI processes (2 hrs). Step 3: Download (5 min)." |
| **Targeted testimonial** | Objection-specific proof near the concern | Testimonial about easy implementation next to "How it works" section |

### Top Objections by Vertical

| Vertical | Top Objections | Handling Patterns |
|----------|---------------|------------------|
| **Lead Gen** | "Is this worth the retainer?" "How long until results?" "What if it doesn't work?" | ROI calculator, timeline with milestones, performance guarantee |
| **SaaS** | "Is it hard to set up?" "What about my existing tools?" "What if my team doesn't adopt it?" | Setup time promise, integration list, adoption stats/onboarding support |
| **Ecommerce** | "Is the quality good?" "What if it doesn't fit?" "Is shipping fast?" | Review highlights, return policy, shipping timeline |

## Section 6: Urgency/Scarcity Patterns

### Urgency Patterns

| Pattern | Structure | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Limited time offer** | "[Discount] for the next [timeframe]" | "60% off for the next 37 hours" |
| **Progressive deadline** | Price increases over time | "Early bird: €200. After May 15: €249. After May 25: €299" |
| **Time-sensitive bonus** | Extra value that expires | "First 25 buyers get a 1-on-1 coaching call" |
| **Deadline-driven event** | Tied to external date | "Masterclass starts June 10th. Registration closes Friday" |

### Scarcity Patterns

| Pattern | Structure | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Limited quantity** | "[Number] remaining" | "Only 8 spots left in the June cohort" |
| **Exclusive access** | Waitlist or application | "We onboard 5 new clients per month" |
| **Founding member pricing** | Locked rate for early adopters | "Founding member pricing locked for life" |

### Rules for Legitimate Urgency

- Explain why the limitation exists (builds credibility)
- Use specific numbers and dates, not "expires soon"
- Never fake scarcity — countdown timers that reset destroy trust permanently
- Place after value and trust are established

## Section 7: Final CTA Patterns

### Final Section Structure

| Element | Purpose | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Value recap headline** | Summarize the full offer | "Everything You Need to Scale Your Pipeline" |
| **Bullet summary** | 3-5 key value points | Key benefits, what's included, guarantee |
| **Primary CTA** | Largest, most prominent on the page | "Get My Free Growth Plan" |
| **Risk-removal microcopy** | Final reassurance | "No credit card. No commitment. Just results." |
| **Urgency reminder** | Last push if applicable | "Only 3 spots left this month" |

### Final CTA by Vertical

| Vertical | Recap Focus | CTA Emphasis |
|----------|-----------|--------------|
| **Lead Gen** | What they get + what happens next | "Book My Strategy Call — here's what we'll cover in 30 minutes" |
| **SaaS** | Full feature access + risk removal | "Start My Free Trial — full access, no credit card, cancel anytime" |
| **Ecommerce** | Product value + guarantee + urgency | "Order Now — free shipping, 60-day returns, ships today" |
