# CTA Patterns Reference

Agent-readable reference with 5 CTA types, microcopy patterns, and placement strategy for landing pages across Lead Gen, SaaS, and Ecommerce verticals.

---

## CTA Fundamentals

### Four CTA Principles

| Principle | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **Value-focused** | Emphasize what they get | Describe what they do |
| **First-person** | "Get my free assessment" | "Get your free assessment" |
| **Specific** | Include what happens next | Use vague action words |
| **Contrasting** | Button visually pops | Button blends into page |

### Primary vs Secondary CTAs

| | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|--|-------------|---------------|
| **Purpose** | Drive the main conversion action | Offer a lower-commitment alternative |
| **Prominence** | Largest, most contrasting button | Smaller, less prominent |
| **Commitment** | Higher (buy, sign up, book) | Lower (download, watch, learn more) |
| **Usage** | Always present, repeated throughout | Optional, max 1, must advance same goal |

---

## The 5 CTA Types

| Type | Purpose | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1. Value-first | Lead with what they receive | High-value offers, free trials, consultations |
| 2. Action-driven | Direct command with benefit | Ecommerce purchases, simple signups |
| 3. Risk-removal | Neutralize hesitation | High-ticket offers, skeptical audiences |
| 4. Urgency-based | Create time pressure | Promotions, limited offers, launch events |
| 5. Low-commitment | Reduce friction with easy first step | Cold traffic, complex solutions, long sales cycles |

### CTA Type by Awareness Level

| Awareness | Primary CTA Type | Secondary CTA Type |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Unaware** | Low-commitment | — |
| **Problem Aware** | Low-commitment | — |
| **Solution Aware** | Value-first | Low-commitment |
| **Product Aware** | Action-driven or Value-first | Low-commitment |
| **Most Aware** | Urgency-based or Action-driven | Risk-removal |

## Type 1: Value-First CTAs

**Formula:** `Get/Claim/Access + [specific deliverable]`

### Lead Gen
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Get My Free Audit" | "Takes 15 minutes. Zero commitment." | Specific deliverable, risk removal in microcopy |
| "Claim My Growth Plan" | "Custom recommendations for your business" | First-person ownership, personalized promise |
| "Access the Framework" | "The same system used by 200+ agencies" | Social proof in microcopy reinforces value |

### SaaS
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Start My Free Trial" | "No credit card required. Cancel anytime." | Double risk removal |
| "Get My Demo" | "See it in action. 15 minutes, live walkthrough." | Sets clear expectation |
| "Access All Features Free" | "14 days of full access. No strings attached." | Full access promise |

### Ecommerce
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Get Mine Now" | "Free shipping on all orders" | Urgency + friction removal |
| "Claim My Discount" | "Save 20% today only" | First-person + urgency |
| "Get My Bundle" | "Save €50 when you buy the set" | Specific savings amount |

## Type 3: Risk-Removal CTAs

**Formula:** `[Action] + [guarantee or safety language]`

### Lead Gen
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Try It Risk-Free" | "Cancel anytime. No long-term contracts." | Addresses "stuck" objection |
| "Get Started With Zero Risk" | "If it doesn't work, you don't pay. Period." | Absolute guarantee |
| "Book My Free Consultation" | "No sales pressure. Just answers." | Addresses consultation aversion |

### SaaS
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Start Free, Upgrade Later" | "No credit card. No commitment. No catch." | Triple negation of risk |
| "Try Before You Buy" | "Full access for 14 days. Decide after." | Decision delay reduces pressure |

### Ecommerce
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Try It for 100 Nights" | "Don't love it? Full refund, free return pickup." | Extended trial + effortless return |
| "Order With Free Returns" | "Changed your mind? Send it back, no questions asked." | Pre-answers the return concern |

| "Register Before Friday" | "Early bird pricing ends at midnight" | Specific deadline |

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Get 60% Off Now" | "Annual plan discount ends in 37 hours" | Specific countdown |
| "Join the Beta" | "Limited to 500 users. 127 spots remaining." | Exact numbers feel authentic |

### Ecommerce
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|

| "Get Today's Deal" | "This price expires at midnight" | Time-bound offer |
| "Order for Christmas Delivery" | "Last order date: December 18th" | External deadline |

|------------|-----------|--------------|

| "Calculate My Savings" | "Enter 3 numbers, get your ROI estimate" | Interactive, personalized, low effort |
| "Read the Comparison" | "Side-by-side: us vs competitors" | Helps decision without commitment |

### Ecommerce
| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|

| "View Full Details" | "Ingredients, sizing, reviews" | Information-seeking |
| "Take the Quiz" | "Find your perfect match in 60 seconds" | Interactive, personalized, fun |

## CTA Placement Strategy

| Placement | Pair With | CTA Type |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Hero section | Core value proposition | Value-first or Action-driven |
| After benefits | Value reinforcement | Value-first |
| After social proof | Credibility reminder | Action-driven or Proof-based |
| After objection handling | Guarantee statement | Risk-removal |
| After urgency/scarcity | Deadline reminder | Urgency-based |
| Final section | Full value recap | Value-first (strongest CTA) |

### Secondary CTA Placement

| Scenario | Secondary CTA | Placement |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| High-ticket Lead Gen | "Download the case study" | After benefits section |
| SaaS with long sales cycle | "Watch the demo" | Hero section (below primary) |
| Ecommerce product page | "Add to wishlist" | Near primary add-to-cart |

## Microcopy Patterns

| Pattern | Examples | Best Paired With |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **Risk removal** | "No credit card required" / "Cancel anytime" / "60-day money-back guarantee" | Risk-removal and value-first CTAs |
| **Social proof** | "Join 10,000+ marketers" / "Rated 4.8/5 by 2,300 users" | Action-driven CTAs |
| **Speed/ease** | "Takes 2 minutes" / "Setup in 3 clicks" / "Ships today" | Action-driven and value-first CTAs |
| **Urgency** | "Only 7 spots left" / "Offer ends in 14 hours" | Urgency-based CTAs |
| **Value reinforcement** | "Includes all 300 templates" / "Save €200 today" | Value-first CTAs |
