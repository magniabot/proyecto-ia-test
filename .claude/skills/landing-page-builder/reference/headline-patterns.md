# Headline Patterns Reference

Agent-readable reference with 6 headline types, formulas, and examples for landing page hero sections across Lead Gen, SaaS, and Ecommerce verticals.

---

## Headline Fundamentals

### The 5-Second Rule
A visitor must understand the core value proposition within 5 seconds. The headline carries 80% of that job.

| Length | Verdict |
|--------|---------|
| Under 10 words | Ideal: scannable, punchy, clear |
| 10-15 words | Acceptable if every word earns its place |
| Over 15 words | Too long: loses attention, dilutes impact |

### Headline + Sub-headline Pairing
- **Headline** = outcome or transformation (what they get)
- **Sub-headline** = mechanism or proof (how or why it works)
- Never repeat the same idea in both lines

---

## The 6 Headline Types

| Type | Purpose | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1. Outcome | Promise the end result | Most Aware, Product Aware |
| 2. Problem-agitation | Name and intensify the pain | Problem Aware |
| 3. How-to | Promise a method or process | Solution Aware |
| 4. Proof/statistic | Lead with credibility | Product Aware, skeptical audiences |
| 5. Question | Trigger self-identification | Problem Aware, Unaware |
| 6. Direct offer | State the deal | Most Aware, promotions |

### Headline Type by Awareness Level

| Awareness | Primary Type | Secondary Type |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Unaware** | Question | Problem-agitation |
| **Problem Aware** | Problem-agitation | Question |
| **Solution Aware** | How-to | Outcome |
| **Product Aware** | Proof/statistic | Outcome |
| **Most Aware** | Direct offer | Outcome |

## Type 1: Outcome Headlines

**Formula:** `[Desired outcome] + [specificity or timeframe]`

### Lead Gen
| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Close 30% More Deals This Quarter" | "The sales acceleration system trusted by 200+ B2B teams" | Specific outcome (30%), time-bound (quarter), relevant metric |
| "Get Qualified Leads That Actually Convert" | "Stop wasting budget on leads your sales team ignores" | Addresses quality over quantity, real frustration |
| "Double Your Pipeline Without Doubling Your Budget" | "See how we help agencies scale lead gen profitably" | Growth promise with efficiency constraint |

### SaaS
| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Cut Your Reporting Time by 80%" | "Automated dashboards that update while you sleep" | Specific number (80%), tangible benefit (time saved) |
| "Ship Features 3x Faster" | "The development workflow trusted by 500+ engineering teams" | Outcome-first, social proof in sub-headline |
| "Stop Losing Customers to Churn" | "Predict and prevent cancellations before they happen" | Names the enemy, promises prevention |

### Ecommerce
| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Sleep Better Starting Tonight" | "Premium memory foam mattresses, 100-night risk-free trial" | Immediate outcome, risk removal in sub-headline |
| "Run Faster, Recover Sooner" | "Performance running shoes engineered for distance runners" | Dual outcome, specific audience segment |
| "Professional Results at Home" | "Salon-grade hair tools used by 50,000+ stylists" | Outcome + social proof |

## Message Match Rule

The LP headline must mirror the ad copy that drove the click. Mismatched headlines cause immediate bounces.

| Ad Copy | Matching LP Headline | Mismatched LP Headline |
|---------|---------------------|----------------------|
| "Get a Free Google Ads Audit" | "Your Free Google Ads Audit Starts Here" | "Welcome to Our Marketing Agency" |
| "50% Off Running Shoes" | "50% Off: Premium Running Shoes" | "Shop Our Collection" |
| "Cut Your Reporting Time by 80%" | "Cut Reporting Time by 80% With Automated Dashboards" | "The #1 Analytics Platform" |

For Search traffic: headline should contain the primary keyword or close variant.
