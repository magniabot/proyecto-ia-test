# Headline Angle Catalog — Reference

Read during headline composition. Provides patterns and examples for each of the 7 headline types.

## Headline Fundamentals

**30-character constraint:**
- 25-30 chars: Optimal (maximize real estate)
- 20-24 chars: Acceptable (could be tighter)
- <20 chars: Wasted space

**Headlines work together:** Each should stand alone, complement others, and cover different angles.

---

## Type 1: Relevance Anchor (H1)

**Purpose:** Signal to Google and the searcher that this ad matches their query.

| Method | When | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| Static | Branded, sensitive, complex | "Google Ads Agency" (17) |
| DKI | High-volume, predictable keywords | `{KeyWord:CRM Software}` |

**DKI Rule:** Default must stand alone. Bad: `{KeyWord:Our Products}`. Good: `{KeyWord:CRM Software}`.

## Type 4: Call-to-Action (H6)

**Formula:** `[Action Verb] + [Specific Outcome] + [Modifier]`

**CTA Strength Hierarchy:**
- Weak: "Contact Us", "Submit", "Click Here"
- Medium: "Learn More", "See Pricing", "Request Demo"
- Strong: "Get Your Free Quote", "Start Free Trial Now", "Book Your Free Audit"

| SaaS | "Get Started in Minutes" | 22 |

## Type 6: Social Proof & Authority (H4)

**Formula:** `[Number/Rating] + [Context]` or `[Credential]`

**Proof Types:**
- User count: "Trusted by [#]+ [Audience]"
- Rating: "[Rating]/5 From [#]+ Reviews"
- Volume: "[#]+ [Actions] Delivered"
- Authority: "#1 Rated [Product] on [Platform]"
- Named proof: "[Company] + [#] Others"
- Credentials: "[Certification/Award]"
- Experience: "[#] Years of [Expertise]"

| Vertical | Example | Chars |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Lead Gen | "Trusted by 500+ SaaS Cos" | 24 |
| Lead Gen | "Google Premier Partner" | 22 |
| SaaS | "Trusted by 12,000+ Teams" | 24 |
| SaaS | "4.8/5 on G2 (2,400 Reviews)" | 26 |
| Ecommerce | "23,000+ Happy Customers" | 23 |
| Ecommerce | "4.7/5 (8,400 Reviews)" | 21 |

## Type 7: Risk Removal (H5)

**Formula:** `[Guarantee/Trial] + [Terms]` or `[No X Required]`

**Risk Removal Types:**
- Satisfaction: "Love It or [Outcome]"
- Money-back: "[Period] Money Back"
- Free trial: "Try Free for [Period]"
- No commitment: "No [Barrier] Required"
- Easy exit: "Cancel [Ease]"

## Urgency (Modifier, Not Core Angle)

Add sparingly to any headline type:

| Base Headline | + Urgency |
|--------------|-----------|
| "Start Your Free Trial" | "Start Your Free Trial Today" |
| "Get 20% Off" | "Get 20% Off - Ends Friday" |

**Use urgency sparingly.** If every headline screams urgency, none of them do.

## Price Headlines (Situational)

Only use if price is competitive:

| Example | Chars | When |
|---------|-------|------|
| "From €29/Month" | 14 | SaaS with competitive pricing |
| "Premium Sofas From €999" | 23 | Ecommerce anchor pricing |
| "Free Plan Available" | 19 | Freemium SaaS |
