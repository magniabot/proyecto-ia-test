# Offer Audit Checklist — Reference

Used by D15 diagnostic. Run this checklist against available data to produce a composite score.

## The 4 Pillars

### Value (4 checks)

1. **Real problem solved:** Does the offer solve a real, painful problem? (Not a nice-to-have, but a genuine pain point with consequences)
2. **Dream outcome clear:** Is the dream outcome clear and specific? (Customer can picture exactly what they'll get/achieve)
3. **Customer desire:** Would the ideal customer *want* this (not just need it)? (Desire > necessity for conversion)
4. **Value > price:** Is the perceived value higher than the price/effort required? (Multiple value amplifiers present)

### Uniqueness (4 checks)

5. **Comparison resistance:** Is the offer difficult to directly compare with competitors? (Structural uniqueness, not just "better")
6. **Exclusive claim:** Is there something only this business can claim? (Proprietary method, unique mechanism, exclusive feature)
7. **Value stacking:** Is enough value stacked that price comparison is difficult? (3+ additional elements beyond core offer)
8. **Audience specificity:** Does it speak to a specific audience vs. everyone? (Named ICP, not "anyone who needs X")

### Urgency (3 checks)

9. **Reason to act now:** Is there a reason to act now vs. later? (Deadline, limited spots, price change, seasonal)
10. **Authentic urgency:** Is the urgency real, not manufactured? (Verifiable constraint, not fake scarcity)
11. **Clear communication:** Is the deadline or limit clearly communicated? (Specific date/number, not vague "limited")

### Trust (4 checks)

12. **Risk removed:** Has the risk to the customer been removed? (Guarantee, trial, refund, or "no obligation")
13. **Guarantee present:** Is there a specific guarantee, trial, or refund policy? (Named terms: duration, process, conditions)
14. **Proof of results:** Is there proof it works? (Testimonials, reviews, case studies with specifics)
15. **Credibility signals:** Are there credibility signals? (Awards, certifications, user counts, media mentions)

## Scoring

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **12-15** | Strong | Proceed to angle extraction (`/offer-maker angles`) |
| **8-11** | Gaps present | Fix gaps before advertising at scale |
| **<8** | Needs work | Redesign offer (`/offer-maker create`) |

## Gap Identification

| Missing Element | What It Signals | Fix Action |
|----------------|-----------------|------------|
| No clear problem solved | Don't understand customer pain | Customer interviews, review mining |
| Unclear dream outcome | Feature-focused, not outcome-focused | Reframe around transformation |
| Low perceived value | Price/effort too high relative to outcome | Stack value, reduce friction, reframe |
| Easily compared | Commoditized offer | Add unique mechanism, specialize, bundle |
| Nothing unique to claim | No differentiation | Find or create proprietary element |
| No urgency | No reason to act now | Add authentic scarcity or deadline |
| Fake urgency | Trust-damaging | Replace with authentic constraint |
| No risk removal | Asking too much commitment | Add guarantee, trial, or refund |
| No social proof | Haven't collected it | Start collecting reviews immediately |
| No credibility signals | Haven't built authority | Pursue certifications, awards, partnerships |

## Vertical-Specific Minimums

### Lead Gen
| Element | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| Value | Specific deliverable with perceived monetary value |
| Uniqueness | Specialization or proprietary method |
| Urgency | Capacity limit or time-bound offer |
| Trust | "No obligation" + testimonials/case studies |

### SaaS
| Element | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| Value | Clear time-to-value ("results in X minutes") |
| Uniqueness | Feature or approach competitors can't match |
| Urgency | Pricing lock-in or limited beta access |
| Trust | Free trial (no credit card) + money-back guarantee |

### Ecommerce
| Element | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| Value | Bundle or threshold incentive |
| Uniqueness | Product differentiation or exclusive offer structure |
| Urgency | Sale deadline or stock scarcity (real) |
| Trust | Extended return window + ratings/reviews |
