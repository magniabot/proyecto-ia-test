# Offer Audit Checklist — Reference (Maker Copy)

Used by E01 (Create Irresistible Offer) as a validation gate after offer creation.
Identical content to the auditor copy — kept here so the maker can validate independently.

## The 15-Item Checklist

### Value (4 checks)
1. Does it solve a real, painful problem?
2. Is the dream outcome clear and specific?
3. Would the ideal customer *want* this (not just need it)?
4. Is the perceived value higher than the price/effort required?

### Uniqueness (4 checks)
5. Is it difficult to directly compare with competitors?
6. Is there something only this business can claim?
7. Is enough value stacked that price comparison is difficult?
8. Does it speak to a specific audience vs. everyone?

### Urgency (3 checks)
9. Is there a reason to act now vs. later?
10. Is the urgency real (not manufactured)?
11. Is the deadline or limit clearly communicated?

### Trust (4 checks)
12. Has the risk to the customer been removed?
13. Is there a specific guarantee, trial, or refund policy?
14. Is there proof it works (testimonials, reviews, case studies)?
15. Are there credibility signals (awards, certifications, user counts)?

## Scoring
- **12-15:** Strong — proceed to angle extraction
- **8-11:** Gaps present — fix before scaling
- **<8:** Needs fundamental work
