# Awareness Stage Matrix — Reference

Used by E02 (Craft Offer Angles) for traffic temperature classification and angle prioritization.

## The 5 Awareness Stages

| Stage | What they know | What they need | Temperature |
|-------|---------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Unaware** | Doesn't know the problem exists | Problem identification | Cold |
| **Problem Aware** | Knows the problem, not the solutions | Problem validation + consequence | Cold |
| **Solution Aware** | Knows solutions exist, evaluating options | Your unique approach | Warm |
| **Product Aware** | Knows your product, comparing alternatives | Specific benefits + differentiation | Warm |
| **Most Aware** | Ready to buy, needs the right offer | Offer details + urgency | Hot |

## Traffic Source → Awareness Mapping

| Traffic Source | Typical Stage | Temperature |
|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| Brand Search | Most Aware | Hot |
| Competitor Search | Product Aware | Hot |
| Product/Service Search | Solution Aware | Warm |
| Problem/Symptom Search | Problem Aware | Cold |
| Display/YouTube prospecting | Unaware → Problem Aware | Cold |
| Remarketing | Product → Most Aware | Hot |

## Angle Prioritization Matrix

| Angle | Cold (Unaware/Problem) | Warm (Solution) | Hot (Product/Most) |
|-------|----------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Problem/Pain** | LEAD | Include | Light |
| **Value Proposition** | Include | LEAD | Include |
| **USPs** | Light | LEAD | LEAD |
| **Value Boosters** | Skip | Include | LEAD |
| **Social Proof** | Light | Include | LEAD |
| **Risk Removal** | Skip | Include | LEAD |

**Priority meanings:**
- **LEAD** = Dedicate multiple headline slots (H2, H3, H6)
- **Include** = At least one headline slot (H4, H5)
- **Light** = Optional, in descriptions or H8
- **Skip** = Don't emphasize

## Message Strategy by Temperature

### Cold Traffic (Display, YouTube, Problem Search)
- Lead with: Problem/Pain (identify and validate)
- Include: Value Proposition (introduce the solution)
- Skip: Offer details (they're not ready)
- CTA: "Learn more", "Discover", "See how"

### Warm Traffic (Product/Service Search)
- Lead with: Value Proposition, USPs (differentiate)
- Include: Social Proof, Risk Removal (build trust)
- Light: Problem/Pain (they already know)
- CTA: "See how it works", "Compare", "Try free"

### Hot Traffic (Brand, Competitor, Remarketing)
- Lead with: USPs, Value Boosters, Social Proof (close the deal)
- Include: Risk Removal (remove final friction)
- Light/Skip: Problem/Pain (they know the problem)
- CTA: "Get started", "Buy now", "Start free trial"

## Key Principle

One RSA can't serve all awareness stages. If targeting multiple stages, use different ad groups/campaigns with different messaging emphasis.
