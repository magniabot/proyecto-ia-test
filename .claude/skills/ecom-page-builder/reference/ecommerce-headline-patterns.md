# Ecommerce Headline Patterns Reference

Agent-readable reference with headline types, formulas, and examples for ecommerce page hero sections. Covers both Dedicated LPs (transformation/outcome headlines) and Product Pages (descriptive product titles).

---

## Headline Fundamentals

### The 5-Second Rule
A visitor must understand the product and offer within 5 seconds. In ecommerce, the product image does 60% of this job — the headline supports, not leads.

| Length | Verdict |
|--------|---------|
| Under 10 words | Ideal: scannable, punchy, clear |
| 10-15 words | Acceptable if every word earns its place |
| Over 15 words | Too long: loses attention, dilutes impact |

### Page Type Determines Headline Approach

| Page Type | Headline Role | Approach |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Dedicated LP** | Persuasion headline | Outcome, problem, or offer-focused (transformation) |
| **Product Page** | Descriptive product title | Clear identification with key attributes (not a marketing headline) |

### Headline + Sub-headline Pairing
- **Headline** = outcome/transformation OR product identity
- **Sub-headline** = mechanism, proof, or supporting benefit
- Never repeat the same idea in both lines

## Dedicated LP: 6 Headline Types

### Headline Type by Messaging Angle

| Traffic Source | Awareness | Primary Type | Secondary Type |
|---------------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Display/YouTube (cold) | Low | Problem-agitation | Question |
| Generic product search | Medium | How-to | Outcome |
| Product-specific search | Medium-high | Proof/statistic | Outcome |
| Branded search | High | Direct offer | Outcome |
| Retargeting | High | Direct offer | Urgency-based |

---

### Type 1: Outcome Headlines

**Formula:** `[Desired outcome] + [specificity or timeframe]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Sleep Better Starting Tonight" | "Premium memory foam mattresses, 100-night risk-free trial" | Immediate outcome, risk removal in sub-headline |
| "Run Faster, Recover Sooner" | "Performance running shoes engineered for distance runners" | Dual outcome, specific audience segment |
| "Professional Results at Home" | "Salon-grade hair tools used by 50,000+ stylists" | Outcome + social proof |
| "Your Best Skin in 30 Days" | "The dermatologist-recommended routine with visible results" | Time-bound, authority |
| "Workout Anywhere, No Excuses" | "Portable gym equipment that sets up in under 2 minutes" | Lifestyle outcome, specificity |

### Type 2: Problem-Agitation Headlines

**Formula:** `[Pain point] + [consequence or amplification]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Tired of Mattresses That Sag After 6 Months?" | "Our 10-year warranty means you sleep well for a decade" | Specific timeframe, contrasted with warranty |
| "Another Day, Another Bad Hair Day" | "The styling tool that works in 3 minutes flat" | Relatable daily frustration, specific time promise |
| "Still Paying for Gym Memberships You Never Use?" | "Build muscle at home with equipment that fits in a closet" | Financial pain, solution in sub-headline |
| "Sick of Shoes That Fall Apart After One Season?" | "Built to last 5x longer than typical running shoes" | Specific comparison creates reference point |

### Type 3: How-To Headlines

**Formula:** `How to [desired outcome] + [constraint or qualifier]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "How to Get Barista-Quality Coffee at Home" | "The machine that 12,000 coffee lovers swear by" | Aspirational outcome, social proof |
| "How to Look 10 Years Younger Without Surgery" | "The skincare system dermatologists use on themselves" | Bold outcome, authority endorsement |
| "How to Sleep Cool All Night Long" | "Breathable bamboo sheets rated 4.9/5 by 8,000+ customers" | Specific benefit, proof |

### Type 4: Proof/Statistic Headlines

**Formula:** `[Specific statistic or result] + [context]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Rated 4.9/5 by 23,000 Customers" | "The skincare routine dermatologists recommend most" | Volume + expert endorsement |
| "50,000 Runners Can't Be Wrong" | "The shoe that won Runner's World Best in Class" | Social proof + authority |
| "3x More Cushioning Than Standard Running Shoes" | "Feel the difference from your first step" | Quantified comparison |
| "Over 1 Million Sold Since 2021" | "The bestselling water bottle that keeps drinks cold for 24 hours" | Scale + specific benefit |

### Type 5: Question Headlines

**Formula:** `[Question that the target audience answers "yes" to]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Still Waking Up With Back Pain?" | "Join 30,000 people who fixed their sleep with one mattress change" | Self-identification, social proof |
| "Is Your Blender Struggling With Frozen Fruit?" | "1,500 watts of power. Silky smooth in 30 seconds." | Common frustration, specific specs |
| "Tired of Replacing Cheap Cookware Every Year?" | "Professional-grade pans backed by a lifetime warranty" | Cycle of frustration, permanent solution |

### Type 6: Direct Offer Headlines

**Formula:** `[Offer] + [key differentiator or urgency]`

| Headline | Sub-headline | Why It Works |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| "Buy 2, Get 1 Free" | "Our bestselling bundle, only available this weekend" | Simple math, urgency |
| "50% Off All Running Shoes" | "Summer sale ends Sunday at midnight" | Clear discount, specific deadline |
| "Free Shipping on Orders Over $50" | "Plus a 60-day return guarantee on everything" | Friction removal + risk reversal |
| "Launch Price: $79 (Was $120)" | "Introductory offer for the first 500 orders" | Savings + scarcity |

## Product Page: Title Patterns

Product pages use descriptive titles, not persuasion headlines. The product image is the hero.

### Product Title Formula

`[Gender/Audience] + [Key Attribute] + [Product Type]`

| Product Title | Why It Works |
|--------------|--------------|
| "Men's Waterproof Trail Running Shoe" | Audience + key feature + category |
| "Organic Cotton Oversized T-Shirt" | Material + fit + item |
| "Professional 1500W High-Speed Blender" | Grade + spec + category |
| "Queen Size Cooling Memory Foam Mattress" | Size + key benefit + type |

### Product Page Sub-headline

Below the title, include a 1-line benefit summary:

| Title | Sub-headline |
|-------|-------------|
| "Men's Waterproof Trail Shoe" | "All-terrain grip. All-day comfort. Built for the long run." |
| "Professional High-Speed Blender" | "Smoothies, soups, and nut butters in under 60 seconds." |

## Message Match Rule

The LP headline must mirror the ad copy that drove the click. Mismatched headlines cause immediate bounces.

| Ad Copy | Matching LP Headline | Mismatched LP Headline |
|---------|---------------------|----------------------|
| "50% Off Running Shoes" | "50% Off: Premium Running Shoes" | "Shop Our Collection" |
| "Best Rated Mattress 2026" | "Rated 4.9/5 by 23,000 Customers" | "Welcome to Better Sleep" |
| "Free Shipping on All Orders" | "Free Shipping on Every Order" | "Premium Quality Products" |

For Search traffic: headline should contain the primary keyword or close variant.
For Display/YouTube: headline should match the emotional hook from the ad creative.
