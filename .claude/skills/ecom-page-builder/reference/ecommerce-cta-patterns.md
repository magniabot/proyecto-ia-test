# Ecommerce CTA Patterns Reference

Agent-readable reference with 5 CTA types, microcopy patterns, and placement strategy for ecommerce pages (Dedicated LPs and Product Pages).

---

## CTA Fundamentals

### Four Ecommerce CTA Principles

| Principle | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **Value-focused** | Emphasize what they get | Describe what they do |
| **First-person** | "Get mine now" | "Get yours now" |
| **Specific** | Include what happens next | Use vague action words |
| **Contrasting** | Highest-contrast color on the page | Button blends into page |

### Primary vs Secondary CTAs

| | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|--|-------------|---------------|
| **Dedicated LP** | "Buy Now" / "Get Mine" / "Add to Cart" (repeated 4+ times) | None — one goal, one action |
| **Product Page** | "Add to Cart" (position 3, sticky on mobile) | "Add to Wishlist" / "Save for Later" |

---

## The 5 CTA Types

| Type | Purpose | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1. Value-first | Lead with what they receive | Bundles, discounts, free shipping offers |
| 2. Action-driven | Direct command with benefit | Standard product pages, clear-intent visitors |
| 3. Risk-removal | Neutralize hesitation | High-ticket products, first-time buyers |
| 4. Urgency-based | Create time pressure | Promotions, limited stock, shipping cutoffs |
| 5. Low-commitment | Reduce friction with easy first step | Cold traffic, complex/new products |

### CTA Type by Traffic Source

| Traffic Source | Awareness | Primary CTA Type |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Display/YouTube (cold) | Low | Low-commitment |
| Generic product search | Medium | Value-first |
| Product-specific search | Medium-high | Action-driven or Value-first |
| Branded search | High | Urgency-based or Action-driven |
| Retargeting | High | Urgency-based |

## Type 1: Value-First CTAs

**Formula:** `Get/Claim/Access + [specific deliverable]`

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Get Mine Now" | "Free shipping on all orders" | Urgency + friction removal |
| "Claim My Discount" | "Save 20% — today only" | First-person + urgency |
| "Get My Bundle" | "Save $50 when you buy the set" | Specific savings amount |
| "Get the Complete Set" | "Shoe + insole + cleaning kit for $110 (save $42)" | Full value visibility |

---

## Type 2: Action-Driven CTAs

**Formula:** `[Action verb] + [benefit or object]`

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Add to Cart" | "In stock. Ships today." | Availability + speed |
| "Buy Now" | "60-day money-back guarantee" | Direct action + risk removal |
| "Shop the Collection" | "New arrivals added weekly" | Discovery framing |
| "Add to Cart" | "Free shipping + easy returns" | Friction removal microcopy |

---

## Type 3: Risk-Removal CTAs

**Formula:** `[Action] + [guarantee or safety language]`

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Try It for 100 Nights" | "Don't love it? Full refund, free return pickup." | Extended trial + effortless return |
| "Order With Free Returns" | "Changed your mind? Send it back, no questions asked." | Pre-answers the return concern |
| "Buy Risk-Free" | "90-day guarantee. Free returns. No restocking fees." | Triple risk removal |

---

## Type 4: Urgency-Based CTAs

**Formula:** `[Action] + [deadline or scarcity signal]`

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "Buy Before It's Gone" | "Only 3 left in stock" | Real inventory scarcity |
| "Get Today's Deal" | "This price expires at midnight" | Time-bound offer |
| "Order for Thursday Delivery" | "Order within 3h 22m" | Shipping cutoff urgency |
| "Lock In This Price" | "Price returns to $120 after the sale" | Loss aversion |

---

## Type 5: Low-Commitment CTAs

**Formula:** `[Low-effort action] + [immediate value]`

| Button Text | Microcopy | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| "See It in Action" | "Watch the 60-second demo video" | Video is passive, low effort |
| "View Full Details" | "Ingredients, sizing, reviews" | Information-seeking |
| "Take the Quiz" | "Find your perfect match in 60 seconds" | Interactive, personalized |
| "Compare Models" | "Side-by-side feature comparison" | Helps decision without commitment |

## Express Checkout CTAs

Express checkout reduces purchase friction by up to 80% on mobile.

| Button | When to Show | Placement |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Apple Pay | Always on supported devices | Below primary CTA |
| Google Pay | Always on supported devices | Below primary CTA |
| Shop Pay | Shopify stores | Below primary CTA |
| PayPal Express | All stores | Below primary CTA |
| Buy Now, Pay Later (Klarna/Afterpay) | Products over $50 | Below primary CTA |

Express checkout buttons should be as visually prominent as the primary CTA.

## CTA Placement Strategy

### Dedicated LP Placement (Minimum 4 CTAs)

| Placement | After Section | CTA Type |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| 1. Hero | Product Showcase | Value-first or Action-driven |
| 2. After proof | Customer Evidence | Action-driven |
| 3. After confidence | Purchase Confidence (FAQ/guarantee) | Risk-removal |
| 4. Final section | Complete Your Purchase | Value-first (strongest version) |

Optional additional placements:
- After Product Benefits section
- After Act Now (urgency) section

### Product Page Placement

| Placement | Location | CTA Type |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Primary | Position 3 (Add-to-Cart Action section) | Action-driven: "Add to Cart" |
| Sticky mobile | Fixed bottom bar on scroll past main CTA | Same text, compact styling |
| Express pay | Below primary CTA | Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay |
| Secondary | Near primary CTA | "Add to Wishlist" / "Save for Later" |

## Microcopy Patterns

| Pattern | Examples | Best Paired With |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **Risk removal** | "Free returns" / "60-day money-back guarantee" / "No restocking fee" | Risk-removal and action-driven CTAs |
| **Shipping promise** | "Ships today" / "Free shipping on all orders" / "Delivery by Thursday" | Action-driven CTAs |
| **Stock indicator** | "Only 3 left" / "In stock" / "Ships within 24h" | Urgency-based CTAs |
| **Social proof** | "Join 50,000+ happy customers" / "Rated 4.9/5" | Value-first CTAs |
| **Savings** | "Save $42 today" / "30% off" / "Best price guaranteed" | Value-first CTAs |
| **Guarantee reminder** | "100-night trial" / "Satisfaction guaranteed" / "Full refund if not satisfied" | Risk-removal CTAs |
